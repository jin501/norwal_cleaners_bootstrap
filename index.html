Why Choose us

Quality & Reliable cleaning

Every visit to Norwal Cleaners feels as if you’ve repurchased your clothing. Your clothing will come back feeling brand new! Our high-quality dry cleaning removes the toughest of stains, allowing you to enjoy your fresh, pressed garments.

Comeptitive Prices

We offer very competitive pricing in our industry and we can argue it is some of the best prices you’ll find around the area. Check out our prices!

Eco-Friendly

We use Eco-friendly dry cleaning technology and have moved away from using perchloroethylene (perc) as a cleaning solvent to help reduce carbon footprints. What better way to contribute to your environment!
